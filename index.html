Welcome

A British education, in the heart of Central Asia.

Established in 2002, Westminster International University in Tashkent was the first international university in Uzbekistan — a partnership with the University of Westminster, London, delivering UK-accredited degrees to a new generation of leaders.

For more than two decades we have built a community of curious minds, ambitious thinkers and engaged citizens. Our graduates work across the globe, in the boardrooms, ministries, classrooms and creative studios shaping the region's future.
